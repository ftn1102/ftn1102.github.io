Do your part!

Conditions are getting better, and we will get through this, but this is a list of some things you could do to help fight the virus.

  • Wash your hands frequently
  • Cover your mouth when you cough or sneeze
  • Frequently disinfect surfaces
  • Avoid crowds
  • Keep a distance of at least 2m or 6 feet
  • Please stay INSIDE as much as possible!
  • Donate any Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
  • Contact your local city officials if you can manufacture or design products that can help fight COVID-19
  • Keep yourself busy by finding a hobby, doing exercise, etc.

Good news

Captain Tom Moore's 100th Birthday

Posted by BBC News on April 30, 2020

On April 30th, Captain Tom Moore Celebrated his 100th Birthday. Captain Moore, a war vetaran decided he wanted to raise £1 000 "for the sake of nurses and the NHS we have, because they are doing such a magnificent job." Instead, he ended up raising a whopping £33 000 000. Hurray for Captain Moore!

Bhangra and Bagpipes

Posted by HuffPost Canada on April 6th, 2020

In Canada, Gurdeep Pandher, a part time bhangra teacher joined forces with his neighbour Jordan Lincez, a teacher who knows how to play the bagpipes to create a sensational video that eminates happiness.

Ozone's back in the zone

Posted by Thomson Reuters and CBC on May 1st, 2020

The ozone hole that opened up over the Arctic had been the largest since 2011. Luckily, that hole has now closed and somehow the COVID-19 lockdown has nothing to do with it.

Stressed? Just Play Tetris

Posted by J.D Warren, UC Riverside on Nov 1st, 2018

According to a recent study at the University of California, distracting yourself can be very effective to relieve anxiety. Playing tetris, chess, or even swimming will distract you. Use this in your daily life!
